Archery


The traditional bow can be traced back to 10,000 BC when a taught string attached to a sturdy piece of wood replaced the spear as the primary means of hunting and warfare.

History


Although the use of the bow and arrow as the primary means of hunting and warfare came about around 10,000 BC arrowheads as old as 60,000 - 70,000 years old have been found in a cave in South Africa.

Is So


Despite the advent of gun-powdered projectiles, around 900 AD, archery still remains a means of hunting and has grown into a competitive sport that is popular even today.
